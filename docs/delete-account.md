# Delete your Shabd account and data

Shabd lets you optionally back up your game progress to our servers using
Google Sign-In. You can delete this backup at any time. Your local game data
on the device is always under your own control.

---

## Option 1 — Delete inside the app (recommended)

If you have the app installed and are signed in:

1. Open the **Shabd** app.
2. Tap **Settings** on the main menu.
3. Scroll to the **Back up progress** section.
4. Tap **Delete cloud data**.
5. Confirm.

This **immediately and permanently** removes:
- Your Shabd account record on our servers (linked only to an opaque Google ID — we never store your name or email).
- All saved game sessions for your account.
- Your streak-freeze history.
- Your stored language and difficulty preferences.
- Any Squads you own (and their members).
- Your membership in any other Squads.

Your local game data on the device is untouched. To also clear local data, uninstall the app or go to Android Settings → Apps → Shabd → Storage → Clear data.

---

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app (for example, you uninstalled it but want your cloud backup deleted):

Send an email to **dwiwediaman@gmail.com** with:
- Subject: `Shabd account deletion`
- Body: The Google account email you used to sign in to Shabd.

We will delete your account record within 7 business days and email confirmation.

---

## What data we store

When you sign in with Google for cloud backup, we store on our servers:

| Data | Why | How long |
|------|-----|----------|
| An opaque Google user identifier ("sub") | To link your sign-in to your saved data | Until you delete |
| A nickname (derived from your Google email's local part, e.g. "Rahul") | Shown to friends in your Squads | Until you delete |
| Your game session records: date, language, guesses, win/loss, attempts | To restore stats across devices | Until you delete |
| Streak freeze usage per week | To preserve your streak across devices | Until you delete |
| Preferences: app language, hard-mode flag, tutorial-seen flag | To restore on a new device | Until you delete |
| Squad memberships and invite codes you've created | For private leaderboards with friends | Until you delete |

We do **not** store your Google email, your real name, your phone number, your contacts, your location, or any analytics on what you do in the app.
