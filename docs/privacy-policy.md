# Privacy Policy — Shabd

**Effective date:** 2026-05-01
**App:** Shabd (in.shabd.game) — Daily Hindi & English Word Puzzle
**Developer:** Rahul Dwiwedi
**Contact:** rahul.dwiwedi@decisionpoint.in

This privacy policy explains what data Shabd collects, how it's used, and your rights.

## Data we collect

### Required for the game to work (no opt-out)
- **Local game state** — your streak, current puzzle progress, settings, and theme unlocks. Stored on your device only. Not transmitted unless you sign in (see below).
- **Anonymous app analytics** — install events, daily-puzzle attempts/solves, share taps, language preference, crashes. Collected via Firebase Analytics + Crashlytics. Linked only to a randomly-generated device ID, never to a personal identifier unless you sign in.

### Optional — only if you sign in with Google
- **Email address** — used solely to identify you across devices when you sign in. Never shared with third parties.
- **Cloud-saved game state** — your streak, max streak, theme unlocks, last completed date. Synced to Google Cloud Firestore so you can resume on a new device. You can delete this any time by signing out and using "Delete cloud data" in settings.

### Ads (rewarded video, opt-in only)
- When you tap "Watch ad for hint", Google AdMob may collect ad-targeting data per its own privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- We never show forced or interstitial ads. You can pay to remove ads (planned for v1.1).

## What we don't collect

- We don't collect your name, phone number, address, or contacts.
- We don't access your camera, microphone, or location.
- We don't read your other apps' data.
- We don't sell or rent any data to third parties.

## How data is used

- Game state is used solely to deliver the game (your puzzle, your streak, your settings).
- Anonymous analytics are used to fix crashes, improve gameplay, and identify which features are working.
- Cloud sync is used solely to back up your progress to your own Google account.

## Where data is stored

- Local data: on your device.
- Cloud-synced data (only if signed in): Google Cloud Firestore, region asia-south1 (Mumbai, India).
- Analytics: Firebase Analytics, Google Cloud (multi-region).

## Your rights

- **Delete your local data:** Uninstall the app.
- **Delete your cloud data:** Sign in, go to Settings → Account → Delete cloud data.
- **Stop analytics:** Settings → Privacy → Disable analytics. Crash reporting can be disabled separately.
- **Stop personalized ads:** Settings → Privacy → Personalized ads → Off.

## Children

This app is rated for everyone (PEGI 3 / IARC General Audiences). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The optional Google Sign-In feature is disabled for accounts identified as belonging to children under 13 per Google's family policies.
